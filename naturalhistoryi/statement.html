The Natural History I series explores the roles of academic institutions in the colonization and exploitation of non-western cultures by directly drawing from their collections. These photos, selected from over 150 similar images, are appropriated from the digital collection archives of the Yale Peabody Museum of Natural History and the Smithsonian National Museum of Natural History. The images were taken by scientists during field research and collection expeditions in the West African countries of Djibouti, Equatorial Guinea, and Gabon.
